# AXIS Governance Planning & Tracking Playbook

---

## 0. Purpose & Operating Rule

This playbook governs **planning, tracking, and execution alignment** across
AXIS. It is _not_ a constitution and does not create new authority.

**Hard rule:** _If not sealed, not binding._ Sealed references are read-only.
Planning artifacts are mutable until ratified.

## 6. Final Seal & Governance Closure

This section records the **final sealing procedure** for this playbook. Upon
seal, this document becomes a **read-only planning reference**. Any future
change must be introduced only through a formal, additive amendment and
re‑sealed.

### 6.1 Sealing Checklist (Executed in Order)

1. **Freeze content** — Final markdown source locked; no further edits permitted
   except via formal amendment.
2. **Canonicalize** — Canonicalization applied per Public Ledger Spec to produce
   the canonical byte sequence.
3. **Compute hash** — Canonical hash (e.g., `sha256`) computed from
   canonicalized source.
4. **Prepare ledger envelope** — Ledger envelope populated with document
   identity, hash, and actor metadata.
5. **Apply patch set** — Any required patch set applied prior to append (if
   applicable).
6. **Append ledger entry** — `CORE_FREEZE_SEAL` or `AMENDMENT_RATIFICATION`
   entry appended to the Public Ledger.
7. **Run verification sweep** — `TITAN_AUDIT_RUN` executed and verification
   results recorded.
8. **Update rollup and tracking** — Master Tracking Page, weekly rollup, and
   Official Seal Register updated.
9. **Notify stakeholders** — Governance Secretariat, System Architect Office,
   Titan Engineering, and Office Owners notified.
10. **Archive sources** — Canonical source and read‑only copy archived; any
    legacy duplicates deprecated.
