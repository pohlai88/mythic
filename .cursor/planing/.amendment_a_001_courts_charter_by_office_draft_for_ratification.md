# Amendment A-001 — Courts Charter (Roles by Office, Succession, Tenure)

**Amendment ID:** A-001 **Status:** Ratified **Applies To:** Amendment A-000
(Courts), NexusCanon Constitution v1.0 — Article IX **Type:** Additive /
Procedural Only (No new doctrine)

---

## A-001.1 Purpose

This amendment defines Court roles **by office**, establishing legitimacy,
succession, and continuity. It prevents ambiguity in interpretive authority and
ensures Courts cannot be captured by ad hoc personal authority.

---

## A-001.2 Offices (Defined Roles)

### Office: System Architect

- **Mandate:** Custodian of doctrinal integrity; final signing authority per
  A-000.7.
- **Powers:**
  - Convenes Court reviews.
  - Declares proposal classification (procedural / clarification / constraint /
    cross-layer) for quorum determination.
  - May propose Amendments.

### Office: Doctrine Court Chair

- **Mandate:** Guards Origin traceability.
- **Powers:**
  - Issues Traceability Check verdicts.
  - Publishes Doctrine Interpretation Notes.
  - Hard veto on alien doctrine (A-000.3).

### Office: Integrity Court Chair

- **Mandate:** Guards architectural integrity boundaries.
- **Powers:**
  - Issues Conformance Test verdicts.
  - Hard veto on conformance failure (A-000.3).

### Office: Execution Court Chair

- **Mandate:** Guards constitutional scope boundaries.
- **Powers:**
  - Issues Scope Contamination verdicts.
  - Issues reclassification directives (Constitution vs AXIS vs Titan).

### Office: AXIS Product Steward

- **Mandate:** Represents product-level implications of constitutional changes.
- **Powers:**
  - Required signer when an amendment impacts AXIS.
  - May issue Product Interpretation Notes (non-binding, product-layer only).

### Office: Titan Execution Steward

- **Mandate:** Represents enforcement-level implications.
- **Powers:**
  - Required signer when an amendment impacts Titan enforcement.
  - Owns translation of conformance into gates (post-ratification).

---

## A-001.3 Appointment and Succession

### Appointment

- Each Office SHALL be appointed by the **System Architect Office** and recorded
  in the Public Ledger.
- Appointments SHALL specify:
  - Office holder
  - Start date
  - Term length

### Succession

- Each Office SHALL designate a Deputy.
- If an Office becomes vacant, the Deputy assumes acting authority for up to
  **30 days**.
- A permanent appointment MUST be recorded in the Public Ledger within that
  period.

---

## A-001.4 Tenure and Removal

### Tenure

- Default term: **12 months**, renewable.
- Emergency replacements MAY be made immediately if integrity is threatened.

### Removal

- An Office holder MAY be removed if:
  - Proven violation of A-000.3 veto rules
  - Conflict of interest undisclosed
  - Repeated failure to meet review obligations

Removal requires:

- System Architect + one Court Chair concurrence (Doctrine or Integrity)
- Ledger entry with reason and effective date

---

## A-001.5 Conflicts of Interest

- Court Chairs SHALL declare conflicts before ruling.
- If conflicted, the Deputy SHALL rule.
- Conflict declarations SHALL be logged in the Public Ledger.

---

## A-001.6 Interpretation Notes Publication Rules

All Court Interpretation Notes SHALL:

- be timestamped
- be attributed to a Court Office
- be appended to the Public Ledger
- include the clause(s) referenced
- state explicitly: **non-binding**

---

## A-001.7 Non-Doctrinal Constraint

This amendment SHALL NOT introduce new doctrine or alter constitutional clauses.
It only defines offices and continuity of procedural authority.
