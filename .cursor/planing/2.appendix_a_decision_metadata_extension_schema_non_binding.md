# Appendix‑A — Decision Metadata Extension Schema (Non‑Binding)

**Status:** Informative — Not Constitutional  
**Parent Authority:** AXIS Product Constitution v1.0  
**Scope:** Optional metadata extensions for AXIS Decisions  
**Authority Level:** Non‑binding appendix (schema valve)  
**Operating Rule:** Extensions MAY be used. They SHALL NOT be mandatory unless explicitly elevated by amendment.

---

## A.1 Purpose

This appendix defines a **controlled extension surface** for AXIS Decision metadata.

Its purpose is to:
- enable richer classification and lineage
- support audit clarity without expanding authority
- preserve constitutional minimalism

This appendix provides **schema shape only**. It does not create policy, enforcement, or veto power.

---

## A.2 Extension Fields (Optional)

The following fields MAY be attached to an AXIS Decision. Their absence SHALL NOT affect Decision validity.

### A.2.1 Classification

**Field:** `classification`

**Description:** Descriptive categorization of the Decision.

**Example values (illustrative only):**
- `strategic`
- `operational`
- `compliance`
- `technical`

**Constraint:** Classification is informative. It SHALL NOT alter authority, enforcement, or execution semantics.

---

### A.2.2 Impact Scope

**Field:** `impact_scope`

**Description:** Identifies organizational domains or Offices materially affected by the Decision.

**Examples:**
- Finance Office
- Engineering Office
- Group‑wide

**Constraint:** Impact scope SHALL NOT imply approval routing, workflow, or expanded authority.

---

### A.2.3 Supersession Reference

**Field:** `supersession_ref`

**Description:** References a prior Decision that is corrected, clarified, or superseded.

**Rules:**
- Supersession SHALL be additive
- The original Decision remains immutable
- Supersession establishes lineage, not erasure

---

## A.3 Governance Constraints

1. All fields in this appendix are **MAY**.
2. No AXIS surface SHALL require these fields unless explicitly elevated.
3. Elevation to mandatory status requires:
   - a constitutional amendment, or
   - formal appendix promotion with an effective date.
