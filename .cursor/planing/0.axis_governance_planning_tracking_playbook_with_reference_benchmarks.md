# AXIS Governance Planning & Tracking Playbook

---

## 0. Purpose & Operating Rule

This playbook governs **planning, tracking, and execution alignment** across
AXIS. It is _not_ a constitution and does not create new authority.

**Hard rule:** _If not sealed, not binding._ Sealed references are read-only.
Planning artifacts are mutable until ratified.

---

## 1. Authority Layers (Read-Only Context)

- **L0 — Doctrine:** Immutable, philosophical or constitutional law
- **L1 — Constitutions & Canons:** Binding system/product law
- **L2 — Planning & Extensions:** Executable plans, specs, and delivery
  artifacts

## 3. Reference Benchmarks (Side-by-Side)

**Rule:** Benchmark entries are _index cards_. They summarize sealed truth and
planning hooks; they do **not** introduce new law.

Each reference is expressed as a **complete cluster** across **X / Y / Z** so
that downstream work can plan safely without reinterpretation.

### X / Y / Z Cluster Definition

- **X — Authority & Identity:** What it is, where it sits, and what it does
  _not_ do
- **Y — Required Cluster Outputs:** What must exist together for this artifact
  to be considered complete
- **Z — Enforcement & Planning Hooks:** How it constrains or guides development
  work
