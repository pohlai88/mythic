# Amendment A-001: Courts Charter

<StatusBadge status="draft" size="lg" />

## Summary

This amendment proposes adding Article VI to the NexusCanon Constitution, establishing governance courts for dispute resolution.

## Rationale

The current constitution lacks formal mechanisms for:

- Dispute resolution between stakeholders
- Interpretation of ambiguous provisions
- Enforcement of governance rules

## Proposed Changes

### New Article VI - Governance Courts

#### Section 1: Establishment

A Governance Court is hereby established to:

1. Interpret constitutional provisions
2. Resolve disputes between stakeholders
3. Enforce governance rules
4. Review amendment proposals

#### Section 2: Composition

The Court shall consist of:

- 3-5 elected justices
- Rotating chairperson
- Recording secretary

#### Section 3: Procedures

Court proceedings shall:

1. Be documented in full
2. Follow established procedures
3. Provide written decisions
4. Be recorded in ledger
