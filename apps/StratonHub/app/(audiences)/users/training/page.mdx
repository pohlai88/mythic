# Operator Training

> Guided onboarding and operational literacy for consistent execution

## Overview

This section provides structured training paths for operators entering the
NexusCanon system.

**Purpose**: Ensure every operator has the knowledge required to execute with
precision and maintain audit-ready discipline.

## Training Modules

### Module 1: System Orientation

- Understanding NexusCanon architecture
- Navigating AXIS surfaces
- Role-based access and permissions

### Module 2: Operational Discipline

- Standard operating procedures
- Trace discipline and audit trails
- Error handling and escalation

### Module 3: Surface Mastery

Each AXIS surface has dedicated training:

- [BoardRoom](/users/boardroom) — Executive decision engine
- Accounting — Bookkeeping and ledger management
- Finance — Financial planning and analysis
- CRM — Customer relationship management
- Manufacturing — Production workflows
- Supply Chain — Logistics coordination
- Procurement — Vendor and purchase management
- Marketing — Campaign execution
- Investor Relations — Stakeholder communication

## Certification Path

1. **Foundation** — Complete orientation modules
2. **Practitioner** — Demonstrate surface proficiency
3. **Expert** — Lead operational workflows
