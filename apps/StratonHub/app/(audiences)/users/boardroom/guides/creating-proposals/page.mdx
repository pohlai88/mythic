# Creating Proposals

> Step-by-step guide to submitting proposals through the BoardRoom system

## Overview

This guide walks you through the complete process of creating and submitting a
proposal for executive approval.

**Time required**: 5-10 minutes

## Prerequisites

Before you begin:

- Access to the BoardRoom module
- Understanding of your organization's approval circles
- Required proposal data gathered

## Step 1: Navigate to BoardRoom

1. From the main navigation, select **BoardRoom**
2. Click the **New Proposal** button in the top right corner
3. You will see the proposal creation wizard

## Step 2: Select a Stencil

Stencils are pre-configured templates for common proposal types:

| Stencil          | Use Case                      |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Hiring Request   | New employee positions        |
| Budget Expansion | Department budget increases   |
| Vendor Approval  | New vendor onboarding         |
| Policy Change    | Organizational policy updates |

1. Browse available stencils
2. Click on the stencil that matches your proposal type
3. Review the required fields

## Step 3: Fill in Proposal Details

Complete all required fields:

- **Title**: Clear, descriptive proposal title
- **Description**: Detailed explanation of the proposal
- **Impact**: Business impact assessment
- **Timeline**: Requested implementation timeline
- **Budget**: Financial implications (if applicable)

## Step 4: Select Approval Circle

1. Choose the appropriate approval circle
2. Review the approvers who will receive the proposal
3. Confirm the escalation path

## Step 5: Review and Submit

1. Preview your complete proposal
2. Verify all information is accurate
3. Click **Submit for Approval**
4. Note your case number for tracking

## What Happens Next

After submission:

1. Approvers receive notification
2. Track status in **My Proposals**
3. Respond to any clarification requests
4. Receive decision notification

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot find the right stencil

Contact your system administrator to request a new stencil type.

### Submission button is disabled

Ensure all required fields are completed. Red asterisks indicate missing data.
