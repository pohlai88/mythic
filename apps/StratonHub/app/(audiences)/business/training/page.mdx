# Business Training

> Induction paths and operational literacy for decision-makers and stakeholders

## Overview

This section provides training resources for executives, managers, and
stakeholders who need to understand and leverage the NexusCanon system.

**Purpose**: Equip decision-makers with the knowledge to govern effectively and
make informed decisions.

## Training Tracks

### Executive Track

For C-suite and senior leadership:

- System governance overview
- Decision-making workflows
- Audit and compliance understanding
- Strategic reporting capabilities

### Management Track

For department heads and managers:

- Operational workflows
- Team coordination features
- Performance monitoring
- Approval and delegation

### Stakeholder Track

For board members and external stakeholders:

- System capabilities overview
- Reporting and transparency
- Compliance assurance
- Investor communications

## Key Concepts

### Decision Authority

Understanding who can approve what:

- **Circle-based governance** — Decisions flow through defined circles
- **Escalation paths** — When and how decisions escalate
- **Override protocols** — Emergency decision procedures

### Audit Trail

Every stakeholder should understand:

- How actions are recorded
- How to review decision history
- How to demonstrate compliance
