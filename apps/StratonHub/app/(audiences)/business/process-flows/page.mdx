# Process Flows

> Canonical operational workflows across NexusCanon business domains.

## Overview

This section documents the end-to-end business processes implemented in
NexusCanon. Each process flow defines:

- **Trigger**: What initiates the process
- **Steps**: Sequential activities and decision points
- **Actors**: Roles involved at each step
- **Outputs**: Expected deliverables and records

## Procure-to-Pay (P2P)

The complete procurement lifecycle from requisition to payment.

### Process Steps

1. **Requisition**: Department submits purchase request
2. **Approval**: Manager reviews and approves (or rejects)
3. **Sourcing**: Procurement identifies suppliers
4. **Purchase Order**: PO created and sent to supplier
5. **Receipt**: Goods/services received and inspected
6. **Invoice Matching**: Three-way match (PO, Receipt, Invoice)
7. **Payment**: Approved invoices scheduled for payment

### Key Actors

| Role             | Responsibilities                    |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Requisitioner    | Submits purchase requests           |
| Approver         | Reviews and authorizes spending     |
| Procurement      | Sources suppliers, negotiates terms |
| Accounts Payable | Processes invoices and payments     |
| Receiver         | Confirms receipt of goods/services  |

### Controls

- Approval thresholds by amount
- Three-way match required for payment
- Segregation of duties (requester ≠ approver)

## Order-to-Cash (O2C)

Revenue cycle from customer order to cash collection.

### Process Steps

1. **Order Entry**: Customer places order
2. **Credit Check**: Customer creditworthiness verified
3. **Fulfillment**: Order picked, packed, shipped
4. **Delivery**: Goods delivered to customer
5. **Invoicing**: Invoice generated and sent
6. **Collection**: Payment received and applied
7. **Close**: Order marked complete

### Key Actors

| Role        | Responsibilities                       |
| ----------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Sales       | Captures orders, manages relationships |
| Credit      | Evaluates customer credit risk         |
| Warehouse   | Fulfills and ships orders              |
| Billing     | Generates and sends invoices           |
| Collections | Follows up on outstanding payments     |

### Controls

- Credit limit enforcement
- Shipping confirmation required before invoicing
- Aging reports for overdue accounts

## Record-to-Report (R2R)

Financial close and reporting cycle.

### Process Steps

1. **Transaction Recording**: Daily transactions posted
2. **Period Close**: Subledgers closed
3. **Consolidation**: Entities combined
4. **Adjustments**: Accruals and corrections
5. **Review**: Management review and signoff
6. **Reporting**: Financial statements generated
7. **Analysis**: Variance analysis and commentary

### Key Actors

| Role       | Responsibilities                       |
| ---------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Accountant | Records transactions, prepares entries |
| Controller | Reviews close, ensures accuracy        |
| CFO        | Approves final statements              |
| Auditor    | Validates controls and balances        |

### Controls

- Reconciliation required before close
- Management certification
- Audit trail for all adjustments

## Decision-to-Action (D2A)

Governance process for executive decisions.

### Process Steps

1. **Proposal**: Initiative submitted to BoardRoom
2. **Review**: Stakeholders evaluate proposal
3. **Discussion**: Q&A and deliberation
4. **Vote**: Formal voting on proposal
5. **Ratification**: Decision recorded
6. **Implementation**: Action plan executed
7. **Tracking**: Progress monitored

### Key Actors

| Role     | Responsibilities                     |
| -------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Proposer | Submits initiative for consideration |
| Reviewer | Evaluates feasibility and impact     |
| Voter    | Participates in formal decision      |
| Executor | Implements approved decisions        |

### Controls

- Quorum requirements for voting
- Conflict of interest disclosure
- Decision audit trail
