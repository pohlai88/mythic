# KPI Dictionary

> Canonical definitions for all Key Performance Indicators tracked in
> NexusCanon.

## Overview

This dictionary defines the standard KPIs used across all business surfaces.
Each KPI includes:

- **Definition**: What the metric measures
- **Formula**: How the metric is calculated
- **Source**: Where the data originates
- **Frequency**: How often the metric is updated

## Financial KPIs

### Revenue Metrics

| KPI           | Definition                      | Formula                              | Source     | Frequency |
| ------------- | ------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ | ---------- | --------- |
| Gross Revenue | Total revenue before deductions | Sum(Invoice.Amount)                  | Accounting | Daily     |
| Net Revenue   | Revenue after returns/discounts | Gross Revenue - Returns - Discounts  | Accounting | Daily     |
| MRR           | Monthly Recurring Revenue       | Sum(ActiveSubscription.MonthlyValue) | Billing    | Monthly   |

### Profitability Metrics

| KPI              | Definition                                                  | Formula                             | Source     | Frequency |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ---------- | --------- |
| Gross Margin     | Revenue minus COGS                                          | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue          | Accounting | Monthly   |
| Operating Margin | Operating income ratio                                      | Operating Income / Revenue          | Accounting | Monthly   |
| EBITDA           | Earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, amortization | Net Income + Interest + Taxes + D&A | Accounting | Monthly   |

## Operational KPIs

### Process Efficiency

| KPI              | Definition                        | Formula                             | Source        | Frequency |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ------------- | --------- |
| Cycle Time       | Time from order to delivery       | Avg(DeliveryDate - OrderDate)       | Supply Chain  | Weekly    |
| On-Time Delivery | Orders delivered by promised date | (OnTimeOrders / TotalOrders) × 100  | Supply Chain  | Weekly    |
| First Pass Yield | Products passing QC first time    | (PassedFirst / TotalProduced) × 100 | Manufacturing | Daily     |

### Customer Metrics

| KPI        | Definition                  | Formula                                | Source | Frequency |
| ---------- | --------------------------- | -------------------------------------- | ------ | --------- |
| NPS        | Net Promoter Score          | %Promoters - %Detractors               | CRM    | Quarterly |
| CSAT       | Customer Satisfaction Score | (Satisfied / TotalResponses) × 100     | CRM    | Monthly   |
| Churn Rate | Customer attrition rate     | (LostCustomers / StartCustomers) × 100 | CRM    | Monthly   |

## Governance KPIs

### Decision Metrics

| KPI               | Definition                       | Formula                                   | Source    | Frequency |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | --------- | --------- |
| Decision Velocity | Average time to ratify proposals | Avg(RatifiedDate - SubmittedDate)         | BoardRoom | Weekly    |
| Approval Rate     | Proposals approved vs submitted  | (Approved / Submitted) × 100              | BoardRoom | Monthly   |
| Compliance Score  | Audit findings addressed         | (AddressedFindings / TotalFindings) × 100 | Audit     | Quarterly |

## Reporting Logic

### Data Refresh Schedule

| Source        | Refresh Frequency  | Latency      |
| ------------- | ------------------ | ------------ |
| Accounting    | Daily at 02:00 UTC | < 4 hours    |
| BoardRoom     | Real-time          | < 1 minute   |
| CRM           | Daily at 06:00 UTC | < 2 hours    |
| Manufacturing | Hourly             | < 15 minutes |
| Supply Chain  | Daily at 04:00 UTC | < 3 hours    |

### Data Quality Rules

1. **Completeness**: All required fields must be populated
2. **Accuracy**: Values must match source system within 0.01%
3. **Timeliness**: Data must be available within stated latency
4. **Consistency**: Same calculation across all reports
