# ERP System Overview

> Comprehensive business overview of the ERP system capabilities, modules, and
> value proposition

## System Capabilities

The ERP system provides comprehensive business management across all key
operational areas:

- **Executive Decision Making**: BoardRoom module for fast, accountable
  decisions
- **Financial Management**: Accounting and Finance modules for complete
  financial control
- **Customer Relations**: CRM module for customer management
- **Operations**: Manufacturing, Supply Chain, and Procurement modules
- **Growth**: Marketing and Investor Relations modules

## ERP Modules

### BoardRoom

Executive decision engine enabling zero-latency approvals with forensic
accountability.

**Business Value**: Transform decision-making from days to seconds, with
complete audit trails.

### Accounting

Complete bookkeeping and accounts management.

**Business Value**: Accurate financial records and compliance.

### Finance

Financial planning and analysis capabilities.

**Business Value**: Strategic financial planning and forecasting.

### CRM

Customer relationship management.

**Business Value**: Improved customer satisfaction and retention.

### Manufacturing

Production and manufacturing management.

**Business Value**: Optimized production processes.

### Supply Chain

Supply chain and logistics management.

**Business Value**: Efficient supply chain operations.

### Procurement

Procurement and vendor management.

**Business Value**: Cost-effective procurement processes.

### Marketing

Marketing campaign and lead management.

**Business Value**: Improved marketing ROI.

### Investor Relations

Investor communication and reporting.

**Business Value**: Transparent investor relations.

## Business Benefits

1. **Operational Efficiency**: Streamlined processes across all business
