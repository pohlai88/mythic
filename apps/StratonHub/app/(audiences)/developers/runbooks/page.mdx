# Runbooks

> Operational procedures for maintaining and troubleshooting StratonHub.

## Overview

Runbooks provide step-by-step procedures for common operational tasks. Each
runbook includes:

- **Purpose**: What the procedure accomplishes
- **Prerequisites**: What you need before starting
- **Steps**: Exact commands and actions
- **Verification**: How to confirm success
- **Rollback**: How to undo if needed
